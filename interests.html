interests

In all honesty, the interest is eating. All related interests are just a vehicle for eating. But I also enjoy cooking, going to new restaurants, food photography, and writing about food. I also really like craft beers and ciders and spend way too much time at breweries because there are too many good small batch releases.

Current favorite food: Sashimi. I live for a good omakase night.

Mortal enemy: Beans. It's a texture thing. I'm sorry. They're mushy and grainy. Grits are close up there too but I'm from the south so they get a pass.

I'm a big fan of walking. Doesn't have to be out in the woods or on a scenic trail or anything, but a good bit of that is nice once in a while too. I have a dog, affectionately referred to as Big Dog, and even though he's a lazy bum like I am, he's also a BIG BOY (lab, shepherd mix) and he needs his exercise.

Sometimes we go on hikes, sometimes we just walk around town, sometimes we just do laps around the neighborhood. I like to listen to music or just rant about people stuff to him. Dogs are great listeners.

Outdoor yoga is also great. Except for the bugs. I have not reached the state of inner peace where I can ignore bugs. I don't think I ever will.

Manga + anime. I'm in pretty deep. I watch a lot of seasonals and have read or watched a lot of the classics, and I've got a fair bit of merch to show for it too. I also like gunpla. I don't know anything about Gundam. But gunpla is very fun. Next project: EVA Lobsters.

Video games. Not as much as before because I'm too poor for nice things. Don't currently have a PC that can run anything. Used to be a support main in LOL, but the highest I ever got was Diamond II RIP. Still follow LCS because I live for the fiesta. I have a PS4 and a Switch but the only games I get on release are Resident Evil and random JRPGs that catch my interest.

Writing. Mostly creative writing, mostly prose, sometimes poetry. Sometimes I have a really rough day and just ramble write nonsense until I feel better. Kind of like what this project text is starting to sound like. Uh oh...

Visual art is exceedingly difficult for me. I don't have mental images. At all. Picture a tree. A square, even. A color. Could you do it? I can't! I can't picture anything, so I don't think visually at all. Drawing involves a lot of drawing and erasing and redrawing because I won't know if it's right until it's wrong. Which is awful. But it's still fun somehow. Occasionally.
